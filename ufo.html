EROS: God of Love.

Eros, named after the Greek God of Love, was founded in 1898. It is the first asteroid to orbit around a spacecraft and to have a spacecraft land on it. NEAR (the spacecraft) finally moving into orbit around Eros on Valentine's Day in 2000. It takes Eros 5 hours and 27 minutes to complete a full rotation and 1.76 years to complete one solar orbit.
