Here you'll find the: Top 5 Most UFO Sightings in the World (dating back from 1947 till 2020)

-World People Review (2023)

Please note that these sighting figures differ from source to source.

1.USA

USA is the most popular country interested in ufology. According to the National UFO Reporting Center, there has been over 105,000 sightings and encounters reported since 1947

2.Canada

Canada has over 15,000 reported sightings from 1989. In 2020, there were 1,243 recorded sightings alone.

3.China

China has recorded over 8,000 sightings since 1980. Beijing and Shanghai are known as sighting-hotspots.

4.France

France has recorded 5,068 sightings dating back to 1954. They also hold the title of highest reported sightings in 2017 which was 455.

5.Spain

Spain has a total of 3,650 reported sightings since 1947. The main hotspot fpr sightings are the Canary Islands and the Balearic Islands.
